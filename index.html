Fanmade Tarot Project

Stray Kids SKZOO Tarot

A custom tarot deck inspired by the Rider–Waite Major Arcana, reimagined through SKZOO characters. The card meanings stay compatible with traditional tarot, while the visuals bring in the heart, charm, and symbolism of Stray Kids' SKZOO world.

This website is a simple home for the current card assignments and guidebook text. You can expand it later with full artwork, reversed meanings, a printable PDF guide, or a gallery view for each card.
