How to Use Tarot Cards ✦

These simple spreads are beginner-friendly and work well for daily guidance, reflection, or checking the energy around a situation.

Before any reading, take a moment to slow down and focus on your question or intention. Shuffle the cards in a way that feels natural to you while thinking about what you want guidance on. When you feel ready, draw the number of cards needed for your chosen spread and read them with both the guide meanings and your own intuition in mind.

1-Card Spread — Daily Guidance

A one-card spread is best when you want a simple message or energy check. It is perfect for daily pulls, quick reflection, or asking what you should focus on right now.

  1. Pull 1 card.
  2. Read it as the main energy, lesson, or guidance for the moment.
  3. Reflect on how its message applies to your day or situation.

2-Card Spread — Situation + Advice

A two-card spread helps you understand both the current energy and the guidance that supports you. It is useful when you want a little more clarity around a specific question.

  1. Card 1: What is happening now?
  2. Card 2: What guidance or support will help most?
  3. Read the pair together as insight plus direction.

3-Card Spread — Past, Present, Future

A three-card spread gives a broader view of the flow of energy through a situation. It is one of the most classic tarot spreads and is especially helpful for beginners.

  1. Card 1: Past — what shaped this situation?
  2. Card 2: Present — what is the energy now?
  3. Card 3: Future — where is this leading?

Major Arcana Meanings

The Major Arcana cards represent the most significant themes and lessons that appear throughout a person’s journey. In tarot, these cards often reflect important moments of growth, change, and self-discovery. Each card symbolizes a different archetype or stage of experience, offering guidance about the energy surrounding a situation and the deeper patterns that may be unfolding.

In the SKZOO Tarot, the traditional meanings of the Rider–Waite Major Arcana are reimagined through the personalities and symbolism of the SKZOO characters. While the artwork brings a playful and unique visual style, the interpretations remain compatible with classic tarot reading methods.

Below you will find guidebook-style descriptions for each card, written to be supportive, reflective, and beginner-friendly. Whether you are new to tarot or already familiar with the cards, these meanings can help you understand the message, mood, and guidance each card may offer during a reading.
